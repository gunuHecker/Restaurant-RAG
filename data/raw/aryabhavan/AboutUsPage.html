About

ARYA BHAVAN RESTAURANTS

Arya Bhavan offers a wide selection of South India Dishes for our esteemed patrons. All the South Indian delicacies are prepared by our expert chefs who use South Indian ingredients. Step into a tasteful journey where our chefs bring the flavors of the south onto your tables at Arya Bhavan.
Indulge in South Indian delicacies like the variety of Dosa’s, Malabar paratha, Idli, Vada at Arya Bhavan – one of the top-rated vegetarian restaurants in Karol Bagh. A warm and prompt staff ensures that all your needs will get proper attention. Head to our South India restaurant near Karol Bagh and experience the finest delicacies today.”
